About

IEEE Student Branch, DA-IICT aims to provide an interactive platform for students to develop professional and technical abilities.The Women In Engineering (WIE) affinity group of IEEE SB DAIICT is organizing ' Tic-Tech-Toe 2018 ' wherein you will be making an innovative applications in the given time frame.We welcome and encourage any kind of innovative applications that can have high utility or impact value.

Hacking is building things that you always wanted to have but no one has built it yet. It's to come up with an amazing idea and work tirelessly on it. It is to fail, fail again and fail better. Try out new things and learn while doing that. It's to work together, collaborate and build things that are innovative.

DATES : 6th to 7th October

VENUE : Sac-1 , DA-IICT

Contest Format

In these 24 hours of brainstorming, all the designers, thinkers, programmers and developers will come together to create WEB or MOBILE applications which might pioneer in the near future or are extended versions of the already ruling notions in the app market.

Rules

1. It Should be a mobile Application or web Application
2. Teams can be formed of 3 to 5 people.
3. Originality of the idea should be there. No plagiarism allowed. Coding should begin when hackathon starts. No precoded projects allowed.
4. Participants will have to bring their own laptops and chargers.No apks, software installations, etc. would be made available from the event team.
5. Students will have to present their application after the 24-hour hack duration completes, on the basis of which the winners will be decided.
6. Accommodation, light refreshments and internet connectivity will be provided during the event hours.
7. Any instance of unseemly behavior or of activities indicating lack of integrity and honesty on the part of the students will be regarded as a breach of discipline.
